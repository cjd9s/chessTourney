    The tournament will be held at the Clayton Hotel in Sligo.

    The tournament has a block booking of 50 rooms for which there is a discount of €30-€35. The discounted room rates, which include access to the leisure center and breakfast, are below. Just mention to the receptionist that you are playing in the Sligo Chess Tournament to receive the preferred rate.

    • Single €120
    • Twin/Double €140
    • Triple €155
    • Children supplement +€10
    • Family Room Supp +€10 for Standard 2 Adults + 2 Children
    • 2 Roomed Family Room +€40 2 Adults + 2 Children
    • Large Family Suite +€60 2 Adults + 3 Children
