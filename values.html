Values

It is the Sligo Chess Club's aim to conduct a safe, well-organised, enjoyable, social, and not-for-profit FIDE-rated tournament.


  • Safe
    There is nothing more important to us than the safety of the players. We will follow government recommendations for Covid and have a Civil Defence first aid team onsite.

  • Well-Organised
    We want the tournament to run smoothly to provide the best possible over-the-board experience. This means that we will start rounds on time, all tournament information will be current and available on the event website as well as on screens at the administration desk and in the player rest areas, and the player rest areas will be comfortable and appropriately resourced.

  • Enjoyable
    Ultimately, chess is about enjoyment. Whether you derive joy from the thrill of competition, the satisfaction in improvement, or gratification in simply participating, we all play chess because it's fun. And our tournament will provide the environment to maximise everyone's entertainment.

  • Social
    Although chess is the focus of the event, the social aspect is why we all come together to play. To that end, we will facilitate occasions for players to connect with other players in the provided comfortable player rest areas and at a tournament-sponsored, evening social event.

  • Not-For-Profit
    We do not intend to profit from this tournament. All money taken in player entries and sponsorships will be put back into the tournament in the form of operational costs, prizes, social events, and anything else that might improve the player's experience.

  • FIDE-Rating
    We want to furnish players with the opportunity to either obtain a FIDE rating or to improve an existing one.
