Register

Registration

Fee

The early entry registration fee is €45 until Friday, April 15th. After that date, the registration fee increases to €50.

Determining Your Section

If you have a FIDE rating, you will use that to determine which section you should enter. If you do not have a FIDE rating, you will use your national federation rating. If you don't have a FIDE or national federation rating, you are unrated and so will enter the Challenger section.
Master: >1800
Major: 1100-1900
Challenger: <1200

Irish Players

Irish players must have a subscription for the Irish Chess Union (ICU) to register for the tournament. You can click here to purchase an ICU subscription.

Once an Irish player has ICU membership, they can click here to register for the tournament.

Foreign Players

Non-Irish players can enter through a separate link for foreign players by clicking foreign player registration.

Foreign players should be careful to ensure that the requested information is correct. Ensure that you enter the correct registration fee (€45 or €50), the name of the event is the "Sligo Spring Tournament 2022", the section is "Challenger", "Major", or "Master", Your name should be your first and last name (e.g. Craig DuBose), your date of birth should be in the format YYYY-MM-DD, your email address should be spelled correctly, and please specify the federation for your provided rating (e.g. FIDE, USCF, ICU, etc. ). Also, I will need your FIDE ID which you can obtain from you national federation.

If a foreign player isn't registered with their national federation, they will need to either register with the Irish Chess Union or their national federation to obtain a FIDE ID.
