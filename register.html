Register

Registration

Fee

The early entry registration fee is €45 until Friday, April 15th. After that date, the registration fee increases to €50. Normal registration will end at 11:59pm on Thursday, April 28th. You can register on Friday, April 29th, until 4pm but there will be an additional €10 surcharge.

Determining Your Section

You will use either your FIDE rating or national rating, whichever is higher. If you don't have a FIDE or national rating, you are unrated and will enter the Challenger section.
Master: >1800
Major: 1100-1900
Challenger: <1200

Chess Federation Membership

All players must be a member of a chess federation so that they have a FIDE ID. Anybody may join any chess federation that they wish.

Irish Chess Union (ICU) Players

ICU players must have a current subscription to register for the tournament. You can click here to purchase an ICU subscription.

Once a player has ICU membership, they can click here to ICU register for the tournament.

Non-Irish Chess Union Players (not ICU, but a different federation)

Non-ICU players can enter through a separate link for non-ICU players by clicking non-ICU player registration.

Non-ICU players should be careful to ensure that the requested information is correct. Ensure that you enter the correct registration fee (€45), the name of the event is the "Sligo Spring Tournament 2022", the section is "Challenger", "Major", or "Master", Your name should be your first and last name (e.g. Craig DuBose), your date of birth should be in the format YYYY-MM-DD, your email address should be spelled correctly, and please specify the federation for your provided rating (e.g. FIDE, UCU, USCF, etc. ).

Also, although I don't need it for registration, I will need your FIDE ID which you can obtain from you national federation.

If a non-ICU player isn't registered with their national federation, they will need to either register with the Irish Chess Union or their national federation to obtain a FIDE ID.
