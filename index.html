Message From Tournament Director Craig DuBose

The Sligo Spring Chess Tournament 2022 has now concluded. I would like to thank everyone involved in in making this tournament happen. Events of this magnitude are always the result of serious work by many different people helping in lots of different way. These people are what made this all possible.

The IT Sligo and Sligo Chess Clubs aim to conduct a safe, well-organised, enjoyable, social, and not-for-profit FIDE-rated tournament.

  • Dates
    Friday, April 29th - Sunday, May 1st

  • Venue
    Clayton Hotel Sligo, Clarion Road, Ballinode, Sligo, F91 N8EF, Ireland

  • Format
    5-Round, FIDE-Rated, Swiss Tournament

  • Time Control
    G/90; INC.30 (i.e. 90 minutes with a 30 second increment per move)

  • Sections (FIDE Rating)
    Master: >1800
    Major: 1100-1900
    Challenger: <1200

  • Round Times
    Friday, April 29th: 7:00pm
    Saturday, April 30th: 10:00am & 3:00pm
    Sunday, May 1st: 10:00am & 3:00pm

  • Early Entry Fee (Until April 15th)
    €45
