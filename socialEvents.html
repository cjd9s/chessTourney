Saturday Social

We hope to hold a Saturday night social event for all players at the Clayton Hotel. We are planning on hosting the event in the ballroom which has a dedicated bar. We hope to have one free drink for every player and a reduced price menu available. There will be a screen displaying any ongoing tournament matches followed by the current tournament table and results. We will have boards, pieces, and clocks available for anyone who wishes to play or analyse.

Additional information will be provided when the event is confirmed.
